India's Coal Dilemma — An Energy Security Simulator

India runs on coal: roughly three-quarters of its electricity is generated by burning it. This tool lets you rebuild the power fleet — slide capacity away from coal, add solar, wind, nuclear or batteries — and watch what happens to a single day of demand. The question isn't whether clean power is cleaner. It's whether the lights stay on after the sun goes down.
